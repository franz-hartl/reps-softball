## The Model: Self-Service

1. **First-timers** call or email to get set up
2. **After setup**, book online anytime
3. **On arrival**, swipe in and train independently
4. **No staff on-site** during sessions

This fits the brand: Preparation. Sacrifice. Put in the reps.

---

## The Audience

**Primary:** Moms of tween girls (ages 9-14) looking for authentic softball training.

They need to feel:
- "This is *for* her, not an afterthought"
- "These people know what they're doing"
- "She'll be part of something"
